About TidyUp

What is this application?

TidyUp is an on-line application for planning household chores, where you can assign them to flat residents and track chores fulfillments. You can also compete to see who cleans up the most at your flat!

How was TidyUp created?

This application was created as a bachelor thesis on the Faculty of Electrical Engineering at Czech Technical University in Prague by Antonín Karola. The most up-to-date technologies were used, such as JHipster Yeoman generator, Java 8, AngularJS, Bootstrap and others. The source codes are avalaible online at GitHub: https://github.com/WhyINeedToFillUsername/bp.

TidyUp was expanded into a mobile app as a group project for CS 407: Foundations of Mobile Systems and Applications at University of Wisconsin, Madison. Group members are Antonín Karola, Joe Damchik, Tanksley Tanksley, and Zhenyu Zhang.
